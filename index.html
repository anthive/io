Rules

Bot is a Client that listens for Sim calls on port :7070. Sim is a virtual world where the game take place. Sim will call your bot/client over http protocol with a JSON payload. Payload contains information about current game state (ants, positions, map, etc.,). Base on that information your bot should decide what action your ants should perform. Your bot response is limited to 500ms. Make sure your logic is officiant ;) Sim will perform orders from your bot. Then the cycle repeats until winning conditions are met.
